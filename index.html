💡 Why Choose Random Forest?
Random Forest delivers superior performance for RestaurantIQ due to its ensemble approach, which combines multiple decision trees to reduce overfitting and improve generalization.
  • Higher Accuracy: 94% for ratings and 97% for risk classification vs Decision Tree's 70% and 90%
  • Better Generalization: Ensemble voting reduces variance and prevents overfitting to training data
  • Robust Handling: Effectively handles missing values and noisy data in real-world restaurant datasets
  • Feature Importance: Provides detailed insights into which factors most influence predictions
  • Production-Ready: Ideal for deployment in real business systems where accuracy is critical
✓ Recommendation
While Decision Tree is excellent for understanding decision logic and is faster in prediction, Random Forest is recommended for production deployments requiring maximum accuracy and reliability in restaurant rating and risk predictions.
